QUANTUM BLACKJACK

[--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------]

- Players start with two cards (each represented by 4 qubits)
the table has 2 'shared' qubits

- Players can either apply a gate to their own hand or apply a 2-qubit gate with a 
table qubit and one of their qubits

- Players can also entangle their own hand with another player's (2-qubit gate again)

- At the end we'll measure and see who has the highest hand

- Players may choose the gates, control/target qubits, and angle of rotation for 
rotation gates
