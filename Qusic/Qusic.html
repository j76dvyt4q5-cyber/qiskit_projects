Gate Reference
H — Hadamard
Puts a qubit into equal superposition. All notes in that bit's range become equally likely. Use to randomize a voice or undo a previous rotation.
X — Pauli-X
Flips |0⟩ to |1⟩. Shifts the note distribution toward the upper or lower half of the pitch range depending on which qubit you target.
RY — Y Rotation
Smoothly biases the qubit toward |0⟩ or |1⟩ by angle θ. The most direct way to nudge the probability toward specific notes. Try +π/4 for higher, -π/4 for lower.
RZ — Z Rotation
Rotates the quantum phase without directly changing note probabilities. Paired with H or RY it creates interference — some notes get amplified, others suppressed.
Z — Pauli-Z
Flips the phase of the |1⟩ amplitude. No visible effect alone — combine with H or RY to create interference and subtle tonal tension.
GHZ State
Maximum entanglement across all 10 qubits. Melody and accompaniment become correlated — they always collapse toward the same extreme of their pitch range together.
